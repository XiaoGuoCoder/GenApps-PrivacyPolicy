Puzzle Lab 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

生效日期 / Effective date: 2026-05-20 · 开发者 / Developer: 郭晓

1. 概述 Overview

Puzzle Lab（"本应用"）尊重并保护你的隐私。本政策说明本应用如何处理信息。 This policy explains how Puzzle Lab handles information.

2. 我们收集的数据 Data We Collect

本应用不收集任何个人数据。所有信息仅保存在你设备的本地存储中,绝不上传、绝不传输离开设备。

This app does not collect any personal data. All information is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded or transmitted off-device.

  • 你导入的照片只在设备上处理用于生成拼图,处理完成后不会保留原图副本到 App 沙箱之外,也不会被上传到任何服务器。
  • 游戏进度、成就、每日挑战完成记录、设置偏好均保存在 App 沙箱的本地文件中。
  • 本应用不使用任何分析、跟踪、广告或崩溃报告 SDK。
  • The photos you import are processed on-device only to generate puzzles, never copied outside the app sandbox, never uploaded.
  • Game sessions, achievements, daily-challenge completion records and preferences are saved in local files inside the app sandbox.
  • No analytics, tracking, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs are used.

3. 权限 Permissions

本应用申请以下权限,仅用于声明的功能,不用于其他目的:

  • 相册访问 (Photo Library) — 用于让你从相册选择照片生成拼图。照片仅在设备本地处理,不离开你的设备。 Used so you can pick a photo from your library to turn it into a jigsaw puzzle. Photos stay on your device.

4. 第三方 Third Parties

本应用不集成任何第三方 SDK、不使用任何第三方分析或广告服务、不与任何第三方共享数据。 This app does not integrate any third-party SDKs and does not share data with any third party.

5. 数据保留与删除 Retention & Deletion

所有数据均保存在 App 沙箱本地。卸载本应用即删除全部本地数据,无任何残留。 All data is stored locally in the app sandbox. Uninstalling the app deletes all local data with no residue.

6. 儿童 Children

本应用不专门面向 13 岁以下儿童,但功能完全离线、无社交、无广告、无内购,适合全年龄使用。 The app is not specifically directed at children under 13 but is fully offline, has no social features, no ads, no in-app purchases, and is suitable for all ages.

7. 变更 Changes

本政策如有更新,将在本页面发布最新版本。Updates will be posted on this page.
