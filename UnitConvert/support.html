About the App

Unit Convert is a fast, native iOS unit converter for everyday use: length, weight, temperature, area, volume, time, speed, data, energy, pressure, power, angle, fuel economy, frequency, and currency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are some currency rates slightly different from another app?

Rates are sourced from the public ER-API service and refreshed periodically. Different providers publish at different times, so small differences are normal. Use rates as a reference, not for trading decisions.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes. All non-currency unit conversions work fully offline. Currency conversions use the last successfully cached rates until the next refresh.

How do I clear my history or favorites?

Open the History or Favorites tab and use the system swipe-to-delete action, or remove all entries from the in-app controls. Deleting the App removes all local data.

Does the app collect my data?

No. See the Privacy Policy for details.

常见问题

为什么货币汇率和别的应用略有不同?

汇率来自公开的 ER-API 服务,会定期刷新。不同数据源发布时间 不同,出现细微差异属正常情况。请将汇率作为参考,而非交易依据。

可以离线使用吗?

可以。除货币换算外的所有单位换算完全离线可用。货币换算会 使用上次成功获取的缓存汇率,直到下一次刷新成功。

如何清空历史或收藏?

在「历史」或「收藏」标签页中,使用系统的左滑删除,或在应用 内的控件里清空。删除本应用即可一次性永久清除其所有本地数据。

应用会收集我的数据吗?

不会。详情见 隐私政策。
