How to play / 玩法

  • Tap a bottle to pick it up — its top color becomes the source.
  • Tap another bottle to pour: same top color or empty target only.
  • Solve a level by isolating each color into its own bottle.
  • 点击一个瓶子选中,再点击另一个瓶子倒出顶部颜色;只有相同颜色或空瓶可作为目标。

Tips / 提示

  • Use Undo to take back a wrong move (unlimited).
  • Shuffle reshuffles the current state; Add Bottle adds one extra empty bottle when you’re stuck.
  • 撤销无限次,重排会重新打乱当前局面,卡关时可以加瓶。

FAQ / 常见问题

Does this app use the internet? No. It is fully offline.

Are there ads or in-app purchases? No.

How do I reset progress? Settings → Reset Progress.

本应用需要联网吗? 不需要,全程离线。

有广告或内购吗? 没有。

如何重置进度? 进入「设置 → 重置进度」即可。
