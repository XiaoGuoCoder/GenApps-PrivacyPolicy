关于本应用 / About

Slide Puzzle Master 是一款经典的滑块拼图游戏。看着右侧的对照原图, 滑动方块将打乱的图块还原为完整图片。支持 12 个内置关卡、四档难度, 以及使用你相册里的任意照片作为自定义拼图。

Slide Puzzle Master is a classic sliding picture puzzle game. Look at the reference image and slide the scrambled tiles around the empty space to restore the original picture. Comes with 12 built-in levels, four difficulty grids, and lets you turn any photo from your library into a puzzle.

常见问题 / FAQ

  • 本应用需要联网吗? / Does the app need internet?
    不需要。本应用完全离线运行,不发送任何网络请求。
    No. The App is fully offline and makes no network requests.
  • 我选择的照片会上传吗? / Are picked photos uploaded?
    不会。照片仅在本应用本地读取,从不离开你的设备。
    No. Photos are read only on-device and never leave your device.
  • 如何清除所有进度? / How do I reset all progress?
    打开「设置」标签 → 重置全部进度 (会弹窗二次确认)。
    Open the Settings tab → Reset all progress (a confirmation dialog will appear).
  • 拼图始终能解吗? / Are puzzles always solvable?
    是的。每次开局通过合法滑动来打乱,从根本上保证一定可还原。
    Yes. Each game is shuffled by performing valid slides only, which guarantees the puzzle is always solvable.
