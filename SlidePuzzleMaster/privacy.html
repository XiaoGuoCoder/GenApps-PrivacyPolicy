Slide Puzzle Master 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

生效日期 / Effective date: 2026-05-20 · 开发者 / Developer: 郭晓

1. 概述 Overview

Slide Puzzle Master（“本应用 / the App”）尊重并保护你的隐私。本政策说明本应用如何处理信息。 This policy explains how Slide Puzzle Master handles information.

2. 我们收集的数据 Data We Collect

我们不收集、不上传、不共享任何个人数据。本应用为完全本地运行的离线游戏， 不会发送任何网络请求,所有数据(关卡进度、成就、用户挑选的照片)仅保存在你的设备本地。

We do not collect, upload, or share any personal data. The App runs entirely on-device, makes no network requests, and stores all data (level progress, achievements, and any photos you pick from your library) only on your device.

3. 权限 Permissions

本应用使用系统照片选择器（PhotosPicker)让你挑选照片作为拼图原图。 你选择的照片仅在本应用本地进程中读取与切片,不会被上传到任何服务器, 也不会持久化离开你的设备(应用关闭即释放)。

The App uses the system PhotosPicker so you can pick a photo to turn into a puzzle. The picked photo is read only in-process on your device, never uploaded to any server, and is not persisted off-device.

4. 第三方 Third Parties

本应用不集成任何第三方 SDK、广告、分析、跟踪、崩溃上报或社交登录服务。

The App integrates no third-party SDKs, ads, analytics, tracking, crash reporting, or social login services.

5. 数据保留与删除 Retention & Deletion

所有本地数据存储于设备的应用沙盒中,卸载本应用即彻底删除全部数据; 在“设置”页可一键清除所有进度与成就。

All local data is stored in the App's sandbox on your device; deleting the App removes all data. You can also wipe all progress and achievements at any time from the in-app Settings screen.

6. 儿童 Children

本应用面向所有年龄段(分级 4+),不针对 13 岁以下儿童收集任何信息, 也不包含定向广告或定向行为。

The App is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13, and the App contains no targeted advertising or behavioral tracking of any kind.

7. 变更 Changes

本政策如有更新,将在本页面发布最新版本。Updates will be posted on this page.
