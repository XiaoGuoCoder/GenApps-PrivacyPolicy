Truth or Dare Party 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

生效日期 / Effective date: 2026-05-19 · 开发者 / Developer: Guoxiao Wang

1. 概述 Overview

《真心话大冒险 / Truth or Dare Party》（"本应用"）尊重并保护你的隐私。本政策说明本应用如何处理信息。 This policy explains how Truth or Dare Party handles information.

2. 我们收集的数据 Data We Collect

本应用不收集、不上传、不共享任何个人数据。本应用是一款完全离线运行的派对游戏,所有数据(玩家名称、自定义卡片、收藏、抽卡历史、设置)仅以 UserDefaults 形式存储在你的设备本地,不会通过网络发送到我们或任何第三方的服务器。

This app does not collect, upload, or share any personal data. Truth or Dare Party is a fully offline party game. All data—player names, custom cards, favorites, draw history, and settings—is stored exclusively on your device via UserDefaults and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

对应 App Store "App Privacy" 标签:Data Not Collected(未收集任何数据)。

3. 权限 Permissions

本应用不申请任何系统权限。不访问相机、相册、麦克风、定位、通讯录、健康、蓝牙、本地网络等任何敏感资源。 This app requests no system permissions. It does not access camera, photos, microphone, location, contacts, health, Bluetooth, or local network.

4. 第三方 Third Parties

本应用不集成任何第三方 SDK,不使用分析、广告、崩溃统计、A/B 测试或任何外部服务。无第三方数据接收方。 This app integrates no third-party SDKs—no analytics, ads, crash reporting, A/B testing, or external services. No third-party data recipients.

5. 数据保留与删除 Retention & Deletion

所有本地数据随你的使用而保留。你可以在「设置」内一键清空玩家、历史、自定义卡片、收藏等任何分类数据;卸载本应用会删除全部本地数据。 All local data persists as you use the app. You can clear any category (players, history, custom cards, favorites) from Settings; uninstalling the app deletes all local data.

6. 儿童 Children

本应用面向 17 岁以上用户(含偶发暗示性 / 成人主题内容,如"刺激"难度卡片)。本应用未面向 13 岁以下儿童设计,也不会主动收集儿童任何数据。 This app is intended for users aged 17+ (it contains infrequent suggestive / mature themes in "Spicy" difficulty cards). It is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

7. 变更 Changes

本政策如有更新,将在本页面发布最新版本。Updates will be posted on this page.
