常见问题 FAQ

· 玩法 How to play

「玩家」标签先添加 2 名以上玩家,回到「主页」点击大按钮抽取真心话或大冒险卡片。可在 「设置」中切换中/英文、调整难度、限制每位玩家的跳过次数。 Add 2+ players in the Players tab, then return to Home and tap the big button to draw a Truth or Dare card. Switch language, adjust difficulty, and set per-player skip limits in Settings.

· 数据 / 隐私 Data & privacy

本应用完全离线、无网络请求、无第三方 SDK。所有数据仅保存在你的设备本地。详见 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy。 This app is fully offline, makes no network requests, and uses no third-party SDKs. All data is stored on your device. See Privacy Policy.

· 自定义卡片 Custom cards

在「卡牌库」标签右上角点击「+」可新增自己的真心话/大冒险卡片,选择主题包、难度并填写中英文文本。 可以编辑或删除已添加的自定义卡片。Add your own Truth/Dare cards from the Library tab via the "+" button—pick pack and difficulty, then fill in the text. Edit or delete custom cards anytime.

· 清空数据 Clearing data

「设置」→「数据」分类下提供清空玩家、历史、自定义卡片、收藏的快捷操作。卸载本应用将清除全部本地数据。 Settings → Data section provides one-tap clearing for players, history, custom cards, or favorites. Uninstalling removes all local data.

· 内容警告 Content notice

本应用包含偶发暗示性 / 成人主题内容(主要在"刺激"难度的情侣主题卡片中)。 建议仅 17 岁以上用户使用,且在合适的私人聚会场合游玩。 This app contains infrequent suggestive / mature themes (mainly in Spicy-difficulty Couples cards). Recommended for users aged 17+ in appropriate private settings.
