AutoCare Log 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

生效日期 / Effective date: 2026-05-20 · 开发者 / Developer: 郭晓 (Guo Xiao)

1. 概述 Overview

AutoCare Log（“本应用”）尊重并保护你的隐私。本应用是一款完全离线的车辆保养记录工具，不收集、不上传、不共享任何个人数据。本政策说明本应用如何处理信息。

AutoCare Log respects and protects your privacy. The app is a fully offline car maintenance logbook. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. This policy explains how the app handles information.

2. 我们收集的数据 Data We Collect

我们不收集任何数据。所有你输入的信息——车辆资料、加油记录、保养与维修记录、提醒设置、紧急信息卡——均仅以本地存储方式保存在你的设备上。本应用不联网,不上传任何信息到任何服务器。Apple 的 App 隐私"营养标签"中我们的声明为 Data Not Collected。

We collect no data. All information you enter — vehicle profiles, fuel/service/repair records, reminder settings, emergency card — is stored locally on your device only. The app makes no network connections and uploads nothing to any server. On the Apple App Privacy nutrition label our declaration is Data Not Collected.

3. 权限 Permissions

本应用仅在你主动开启提醒功能时,请求"通知"权限,用于在到期前(如保养、保险、年检、驾照)发送本地通知。该权限由系统统一管理,所有调度均在设备本地完成,通知内容不会发送到任何服务器。本应用不申请定位、相机、相册、通讯录、麦克风等任何其它权限。

The app requests Notifications permission only when you opt in to reminders, in order to deliver local notifications before due dates (service, insurance, inspection, driving licence). The permission is managed by iOS; all scheduling happens on-device and notification content never leaves your device. The app does not request location, camera, photo library, contacts, microphone, or any other permission.

4. 第三方 Third Parties

本应用未集成任何第三方 SDK,不使用任何分析、广告、崩溃上报或追踪服务。零第三方依赖。

The app integrates no third-party SDKs and uses no analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking services. Zero third-party dependencies.

5. 数据保留与删除 Retention & Deletion

所有数据保存在设备本地的应用沙盒中。卸载本应用即永久删除全部本地数据,无任何备份保留在我方服务器(因为我方根本没有服务器)。你也可在 App 内逐条删除车辆、记录或提醒。

All data is stored in the app's local sandbox on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data; we retain no copy on any server (because we operate none). You may also delete individual vehicles, records, or reminders inside the app.

6. 儿童 Children

本应用未面向 13 岁以下儿童设计,亦不有意收集儿童个人信息。由于本应用不收集任何数据,COPPA 等法规对我方实质不适用。

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app collects no data at all, COPPA and similar regulations are effectively non-applicable to our processing.
